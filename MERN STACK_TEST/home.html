ABOUT US

SNS College of Technology, Coimbatore is an excelling institution in the state committed to producing engineers with high calibre and professionalism by accelerating Engineering and Technological education under one roof.The college was established in 2002 by Sri SNS Charitable Trust with a high Mission and noble thought of educating the needy.

The campus is well-structured in a beautiful environment, with the best infrastructural facilities. The college offers13 UG, 6 PG and 5 Ph.D/Research Programs in the leading fields ofEngineering/Technology and Management

Our primary concept is Design Thinking, where this approach to education provides the students to have the academic freedom to understand complex problems and principles through engaged learning activities and helps nurture progressive minds.
